中文版 (Chinese Version)

1. 引言

欢迎使用“安否”（以下简称“本应用”）。本应用由独立开发者（以下简称“开发者”或“我们”）开发。我们非常重视您的隐私。本隐私政策旨在向您说明在您使用本应用提供的“安全确认”及“紧急通知”服务时，我们要如何收集、使用、存储和保护您的个人信息。

下载或使用本应用，即表示您同意本隐私政策的条款。

2. 我们收集的信息

为了实现本应用的核心功能（即检测您的状态并在异常时通知您的联系人），我们需要收集以下类型的信息：

  • 个人身份信息： 包括您的姓名、手机号码和电子邮箱地址。这些用于创建您的账户以及作为发送紧急通知时的发件人标识。
  • 紧急联系人信息： 您主动添加的紧急联系人的姓名、手机号码和电子邮箱。
    注意：您在提供这些信息前，请确保已获得相关联系人的同意。
  • 活动状态数据： 您的签到时间（Check-in Time）和倒计时设置。这是判断是否发送紧急通知的依据。
  • 预设内容： 您预先编辑好的用于发送给紧急联系人的通知内容（如留言、遗嘱或状态说明）。

3. 信息的使用目的

我们将收集的信息用于以下目的：

  • 核心功能运行： 用于计算您的倒计时状态。
  • 发送通知： 当倒计时结束且您未进行签到时，系统将通过短信或邮件服务，向您预设的紧急联系人发送包含您姓名和预设内容的通知。
  • 账户管理： 用于验证您的身份及恢复数据。

4. 信息共享与披露

我们承诺不会将您的个人信息出售、出租或交易给任何第三方用于广告目的。我们仅在以下情况共享您的信息：

  • 服务提供商： 我们可能会使用第三方服务（如短信网关服务商、邮件发送服务商）来执行发送通知的操作。这些服务商仅有权在执行任务所需范围内使用您的手机号或邮箱，且受到保密协议的约束。
  • 法律要求： 如果法律法规要求，或为了保护我们的权利、财产或安全，我们需要披露您的信息。

5. 数据安全

我们会采取业界通用的安全措施（如HTTPS加密传输、数据库加密存储）来保护您的个人信息，防止数据泄露、丢失或被滥用。但请注意，互联网传输没有任何一种方法是100%绝对安全的。

6. 数据保留与删除

  • 保留： 只要您使用本服务，我们就会保留您的数据。
  • 删除（注销）： 您拥有对自己数据的完全控制权。如果您希望删除您的账户及所有相关数据，您可以在App内部的“设置”页面找到“注销账户”或“删除数据”的选项。一旦操作，您的所有个人信息及联系人数据将从服务器物理删除，且不可恢复。

7. 儿童隐私

本服务不针对13岁以下的儿童。如果你是未成年人，请在法定监护人的陪同下使用。我们不会有意收集13岁以下儿童的个人信息。

English Version

1. Introduction

Welcome to use "An Fou" (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). This App is built by an independent developer (hereinafter referred to as "we" or "us"). We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our "Safety Check" and "Emergency Notification" services.

By downloading or using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

To provide the core functionality of the App (detecting your status and notifying your contacts in case of inactivity), we need to collect the following information:

  • Personal Identity Information: Your Name, Phone Number, and Email Address. These are used to create your account and identify you as the sender of emergency notifications.
  • Emergency Contact Information: The names, phone numbers, and email addresses of the emergency contacts you voluntarily add.
    Note: Please ensure you have obtained permission from your contacts before providing their information.
  • Activity Status Data: Your Check-in Time and countdown settings. This is used to determine whether to trigger an emergency notification.
  • User-Generated Content: The preset messages (e.g., last words, status updates) you create to be sent to your contacts.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Operation: To calculate your countdown status.
  • Sending Notifications: When the countdown ends and you fail to check in, the system will use SMS or Email services to send your preset message (containing your name) to your emergency contacts.
  • Account Management: To verify your identity and restore your data.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We promise NOT to sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third parties for advertising purposes. We share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may employ third-party companies (such as SMS gateways or Email service providers) to facilitate the notification delivery. These third parties have access to your information only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

5. Data Security

We implement commercially acceptable security measures (such as HTTPS encryption and database encryption) to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Retention: We will retain your data for as long as you use our Service.
  • Deletion (Account Termination): You have full control over your data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, you can find the "Delete Account" or "Delete Data" option in the App's "Settings" page. Once confirmed, all your personal information and contact data will be permanently deleted from our servers.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.
